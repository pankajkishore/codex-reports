        Weather and packing

        Cold dawns, warm afternoons

        Current West Yellowstone forecast guidance spans roughly 3-24°C. Yellowstone microclimates vary, so keep layers in the Tesla.

        • Warm fleece or light insulated layer for 4:45-6:00 AM starts.
        • Breathable daytime shirt, sun hat, sunglasses, and sunscreen.
        • Rain shell every day; Wednesday and Friday have the higher shower signal.
        • Supportive walking shoes for geyser boardwalks and canyon stairs.
        • Water, snacks, binoculars, bear spray knowledge, and offline maps.
